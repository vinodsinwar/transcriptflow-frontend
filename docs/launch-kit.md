## Copy blocks (reuse everywhere)

**Name:** TranscriptFlow

**Tagline (≤60 chars):**
> Free YouTube transcripts — with translation, SRT, PDF & Word

**Alternative taglines:**
- Turn any YouTube video into text in seconds
- YouTube to text, subtitles, and translations — free, no signup

**50-word description:**
> TranscriptFlow turns any YouTube video into a clean, timestamped transcript in seconds. Click a timestamp to jump into the video, translate the transcript into the languages YouTube offers, and download it as TXT, SRT, VTT, PDF, or Word. Free, unlimited, no signup.

**100-word description:**
> TranscriptFlow is a free YouTube transcript generator. Paste any video link and get the full timestamped transcript in seconds — including videos with only auto-generated captions. Every timestamp is clickable and jumps to that moment in the video. Translate the transcript into any language the video supports, then export it your way: plain text, SRT or VTT subtitle files for editors and players, or polished PDF and Word documents named after the video. There are no accounts, no usage limits, and no stored data — each request is processed independently. Built for students, journalists, content creators, and accessibility.

**Category/topic tags:** productivity, video, accessibility, writing, education, free-tools
